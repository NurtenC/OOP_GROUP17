Detailed Description

Manages users, their expenses, incomes, and budgets.

The ExpenseTracker class provides functionalities to register and authenticate users, manage their expenses and incomes, track budgets, and handle date and time-related operations. It maintains a collection of users and ensures that each user's financial data is properly managed.
